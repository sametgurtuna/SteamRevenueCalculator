Tax Rates

Turkey Tax:

In Turkey, tax rate is generally 20%.


United States Tax:

Developers may be based in different countries and pay income taxes based on a myriad of circumstances. There is also VAT, that is included in the price in a lot of countries, and can be 5% to 25% depending on the country. So I've just picked a rough number of 30% as the default, and I will add the ability to fine-tune it in the future.


Steam Cut:

Steam takes its 30% cut, although some developers may have negotiated a smaller cut, for example for exclusivity. But this number is probably the standard for indie game developers.

Boxleiter Method

The Boxleiter method is a statistical method used for analyzing the distribution of a dataset.

It is commonly used to determine the normal distribution of a dataset and uncover specific trends or patterns.

In the Steam Revenue Calculator, this method allows users to estimate revenue based on either the number of sales or the number of reviews.
